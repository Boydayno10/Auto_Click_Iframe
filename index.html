Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection and Usage:

This Extension was developed with your privacy in mind. It does not collect, store, monitor, transmit, or share any personally identifiable data or browsing information with the developer or third parties. All user-entered settings (such as XPaths, fill values, intervals, and repetition options) are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (via chrome.storage.local). This information is never sent to external servers or accessed by any other entity.

2. Extension Permissions:

The permissions requested by the Extension (storage, scripting, activeTab, tabs, webNavigation, contextMenus, ) are strictly necessary for its core purpose: automating clicks and form filling on web pages. None of these permissions are used to collect data unrelated to the functionality of the Extension.

3. Data Security:

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your browser and operating system protections. The Extension does not implement network security mechanisms because no data is transmitted.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
